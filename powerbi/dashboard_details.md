## Dashboard Overview

This dashboard was built to analyze insurance policy performance, claim trends and customer demographics using 10,000 insurance records.

The dashboard contains 2 pages.

### Page 1: Executive Dashboard

KPIs:

- Total Premium Amount
- Total Coverage Amount
- Total Claim Amount

Visualizations:

- Premium Amount by Policy Type
- Active vs Inactive Policies
- Number of Claims by Claim Status
- Claim Amount by Age Group
- Policy Summary Table

Filters:

- Policy Number
- Claim Number
- Customer ID

---

### Page 2: Data Explorer

Displays detailed insurance records for analysis.

Columns:

- Policy Number
- Customer ID
- Claim Number
- Age
- Gender
- Coverage Amount
- Premium Amount
- Policy Start Date
- Policy End Date
- Policy Type
- Claim Status
- Claim Date
- Claim Amount
- Age Group
- Active/Inactive

---

## Key Insights

- Travel insurance generated the highest premium revenue.
- Active policies exceeded inactive policies.
- Adults generated the highest claim amount.
- Rejected claims were the most frequent claim status.
- Travel and Health policies contributed significantly to total coverage.
- Young adults generated comparatively lower claim amounts.
